# Promote
This is a promote application, that I have built for one of my university Projects. the Aim of this app is to promote local business. 
The app is designed to help local small businesses promote their business to people who are not able to reach their physical location. As we all know, there are many businesses that have great potential but their location prevents them from being seen by potential customers. 
My app aims to change this by providing businesses with an opportunity to promote their business on the app, and share their location, products, and other relevant information.
While there are other similar apps such as Uber and Deliveroo, my app is different in that it focuses solely on promoting the business, in hopes of people going to the actual shop or donating to the business. The app is designed for people of all genders and age groups of 16+.
This app is not only designed to help local businesses, but also to bring people together during these challenging times. With the pandemic affecting so many people, the app encourages social interaction, as people use the app to interact with local businesses and support their community.
